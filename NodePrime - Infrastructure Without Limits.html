Senior Systems Engineer

UNIX/Linux Python Perl PHP Bash TCP/IP

San Francisco, CA   apply


If you are excited about systems engineering, this is an opportunity to work on this technology full-time. As a systems engineer, you'll be responsible for ensuring that NodePrime’s infrastructure is available, reliable, and fast, at all times. You must be able to work independently and multi-task among several concurrent problems, perform triage and prioritization as necessary through the exercise of discretion and independent judgment. This person is a self-starter with a strong sense of responsibility and problem ownership who can commit to driving issues to completion; someone who can adapt quickly, and is capable of compiling together working solutions across a broad technology stack and working with engineering teams on long-term fixes.

Responsibilities
  • Apply in-depth systems analysis to project-based work, design solutions to issues, and evaluate their effectiveness.
  • Strategize with the engineering team on complex problems, and make decisions and recommendations about systems improvements after analyzing possible courses of conduct.
  • Design and improve automation and tools for systems management, including development of scripts for our automated remediation framework, and build tools to help service owners and fellow engineers maintain the infrastructure.
  • Independently learn new technologies and master the NodePrime infrastructure so that you can provide 'full stack' diagnostics, when necessary, to help determine the root cause of internal problems.
  • Communicate effectively with fellow engineers, and describe problems precisely with sufficient detail that you can hand-off an ongoing problem to another team or a peer for completion.
  • Accurate information transfer and positive engagement with other teams is a vital responsibility.
  • Perform periodic on-call duty as part of a team maintaining the availability and performance of the NodePrime Infrastructure and APIs, as well as the various internal services and systems that these core interfaces depend on.
  • Perform software installations and documentation.
  • Manage configuration changes for the deployed systems.

Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer science or related field.
  • Experience with distributed UNIX/Linux systems administration and performance tuning.
  • Solid understanding of TCP/IP networking and switching.
  • Proficiency in Python, PHP, Perl, or Bash for operations scripts and text processing is expected. Python experience is preferred.
  • Troubleshooting skills that range from diagnosing low-level hardware issues to large-scale failures within datacenter clusters.
  • Ability to work independently.
  • Experience with network management systems and network monitoring tools such as Nagios, Ganglia, and/or Cacti.
  • Prior experience in a fast-paced environment, resolving multiple interrupt-driven priorities simultaneously.
  • Experience with Incident management.
  • Experience with load balancing, storage and clustering technologies.
